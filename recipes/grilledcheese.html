Grilled Cheese

Description

Bread. Cheese. Heat. Meat? No Meat*.

*Sometimes meat

Ingredients:

  • Artisinally baked, small batch Wonderbread(tm) from your local Walgreens.
  • Freshly churned, risen, and homogenized American cheese.
  • Butter, of any national variety, though preferably Canadian.

Steps

  1. Find two of your finest slices of bread; lay them out parallel to eachother on a plate
  2. Give them a good talking to - let them know that they aren't all that, but soon they shall be
  3. Remove the shell from 4 slices of american cheese
  4. In culinary circles, this process is known as undressing the cheese
  5. Using the classically derived stitching techniques of ancient Celtic art, softly but intentionally entertwine your cheese slices with eachother
  6. Over an open flame, bring a skillet to medium heat, and melt your butter within
  7. Note: if an open flame is unavailable, an electric stovetop element will work in a pinch
  8. Bring bread to bread in a glorious union, making what is known as an "Grilled Cheese Tartare"
  9. Chuck that thang into your pan, and cook until golden brown on one side
  10. Don't mooooove it! Let it get nice and golden.

  11. Flip it over before it burns
  12. Remove from pan once cheese has melted, and slice diagonally
  13. Garnish with a puddle of warmed half n half, and freshly plucked stalks of wheat. Serve with a side of filet mignon, RARE
