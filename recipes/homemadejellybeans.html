Homemade Jelly Beans

Description

Picture this if you will:

The curtain rises to show a small man, beaten down by life, sitting with his head in his hands at his kitchen table.

"Why!" the man cries, "Why can't I have my jelly beans? What have I done to deserve this?"

Achingly, the man raises his hands, following them hungrily with his eyes, towards the clock hanging on his wall.

"The night is young, the hour is but 2 AM, yet all the shops are closed! My heart burns and bleeds for jellybeans, but alas, I am unable to purchase those sweet trifles of heavenly joy!"

If only our hero knew, just how easily he could synthesize these delights.

Ingredients:

  • One can of beans, any variety
  • One jar of jelly, though jam will work in a pinch

Steps

  1. Using a fine mesh sieve, rinse the goo off of your beans
  2. Once adequately degreased, place your cleansed and sanctified beans into a large bowl
  3. Empty contents of jelly jar into bowl
  4. With freshly washed hands, massage the jelly onto and into the beans
  5. Consume
  6. Regret your decisions
  7. Drive to the 24 hour gas station for cheap wine and jellybeans

Bon Appetit!
